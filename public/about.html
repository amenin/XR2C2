About Us

XR²C², launched in 2021 through the IDEX (Initiative d'Excellence) program of Université Côte d'Azur, stands for eXtented Reality Research Center and Creativity.

It offers a research and creation structure for the analysis of immersive practices, the implementation of innovative technologies and programs in the field of XR and its applications, hosting artists and programs in research-creation for immersive sound creation and spatial design, ludic practices, XR scenarization and design, innovative storytelling, new curatorial and museographical practices, archives, training and rehabilitation programs, and immersive heritage.

This website was developed and structured by Steven ESSAM as part of a research project conducted in collaboration with I3S and DS4H at the University Côte d'Azur. His university project, titled "Design and development of a tool for the preservation, archiving, and exhibition of projects in extended realities," is part of the Tutorships Project for the Master 1 in Computer Science for the academic year 2023/2024.

Supervised by Aline MENIN & Marco WINCKLER, this work has led to the development of innovative solutions for the preservation and consultation of immersive projects and artworks within the XR2C2 - eXtented Reality Research Center and Creativity.
